ABOUT ME

I am an aspiring Engineer currently completing a BA.Sc in Mechatronics Engineering at the University of British Columbia. Born in Winnipeg, Canada, I moved to Vancouver to attend one of the top engineering schools in Canada. My Engineering interests involve software development, robotics, and controls engineering. I have 4 years of applied experience in C, C#, and Python, and a working knowledge of web design languages (HTML/CSS/JS). Through internships and several personal projects, I have gained diverse experience in the fields of mechanical design, firmware development / debugging, system modelling, and software design. In my free time, I enjoy golfing, cooking, and watching movies. I am eager to learn as much about the Engineering world as possible and hope to work at companies that help make the world a more functional place.
